The Story

Just a dad figuring it
out — one post at a time.

Hey Modern Dad started as a simple idea: what if dads talked honestly about the chaos, joy, and total confusion that comes with raising kids in 2024? No highlight reel. No advice from someone who has it all together. Just raw, relatable content from a dad in the trenches.

Our mission is simple — helping dads navigate parenting with humor and honesty. Whether you're a brand-new dad panicking at 3am, or a seasoned pro who still feels like he's winging it (spoiler: we all are), this is your corner of the internet.

Dad Tips

Quick wins for every dad

Practical, proven advice from a dad who's been there (and survived).

🌙

Sleep Training Sanity

Consistency beats perfection every time. Pick a method, stick with it for two weeks, and remember — this phase ends. You'll sleep again.

Read more
🎯

Screen Time Balance

The goal isn't zero screens — it's intentional screens. Watch with them, ask questions, and make it a shared experience when you can.

Read more
💬

Talk Early, Talk Often

Narrate your day out loud — even to a newborn. Language development starts way earlier than most people think.

Read more
🤸

Active Play Daily

20 minutes of rough-and-tumble play a day does more for behavior than any parenting book. Kids need to move their bodies — and so do you.

Read more
🧘

Dad Self-Care Isn't Selfish

You can't pour from an empty cup. Ten minutes of something just for you each day is a parenting investment, not a luxury.

Read more
❤️

Repair After Conflict

Lost your cool? That's okay — it's human. What matters is the repair: apologies, connection, and showing kids that relationships can bounce back.
